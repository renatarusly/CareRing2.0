# CareRing

CareRing is an AI companion system for elderly people when their caregiver or family members are busy. CareRing also allows caregivers or family members to stay in touch with the eldery. 

The app allows voice calls with AI, which checks in and asks daily questions such as how was their day, reminds them about taking medications and at the end gives a summary about the call to the caregiver or alerts them of anything wrong. Using CareRing, the caregiver or the eldery can schedule a time for them to call each other. 

The goal is to enhance human connection experiences between family members. 

---

## Problem

Nowadays, keeping in contact with family members can be difficult especially if people are working all day. This is especially true for family members who may live in different countries in different timezones. Elderly people can also become lonely without their family. 

---

## Solution

CareRing aims to create a companion that takes care of the elderly in place of their caregiver or family members. This AI companion asks check in questions to make sure that the elderly person is doing well. It also reminds the elderly about any medications that they need to take.

CareRing makes contacting with your family members quicker and easier. The app allows the caregiver or family member to schedule a time for when they want to connect with their family and the app sends a notification to remind both parties. 

---

## Features

- **AI Companion**
  AI voiechat that asks questions for check up on elderly.

- **Summary**
  AI summary of the key points in the AI Companion call that the caregiver can check on. 

- **Alert**
  Alert notification to caregiver if something is wrong. 

- **Schedule call**
  Allows user to schedule a call between caregiver and elderly.

---

## Target Users

People who are working full-time or people who are not living with their elderly family members.

## How It Works

1. User chooses whether they are a caregiver or an elderly person.
2. Connect with your caregiver or elderly person using code.
   
Caregiver:
3. Set AI Companion calls. User can choose how many calls made per day and what type of questions asked during the call.
4. AI Summary. User can see the summary of the AI Companion calls made during the day. 
5. Can set human calls. User can schedule a time for when they want to call with the elderly person. 

Elderly person:
3. Call with AI Companion. 
4. Call with caregiver or family.
